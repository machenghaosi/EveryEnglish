Daily Skills

日常英语只先练五种能力

先不用追求“高级”，先让自己在真实生活里能接住对话、说出需求、继续往下聊。

1

提出请求

Could I...? / Would it be possible to...?

2

解释原因

Something came up, so... / The thing is...

3

确认信息

Just to confirm... / Did you mean...?

4

临时改计划

Can we move it to...? / Would later still work?

5

自然收尾

Thanks for understanding. / I appreciate it.
